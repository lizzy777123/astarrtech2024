Founders education / qualifications

ACCA

Established in 1904, ACCA has grown into a prestigious global accounting body, emphasizing professional ethics, strategic insight, and financial expertise. Through rigorous exams and practical experience, it prepares members for impactful careers in finance and accounting.

ACCA Website Link

IET

The Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET), established in 1871, merged the Institution of Electrical Engineers and the Institution of Incorporated Engineers in 2006. With over 158,000 members in 153 countries, the IET sets professional standards and supports the engineering community globally.

IET Website Link

Brunel University

Brunel University London, named after the Victorian engineer Isambard Kingdom Brunel, is renowned for its strong research, engineering heritage, and focus on providing education that equips students with practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge. It's committed to academic excellence and innovation.

Brunel University Website Link

University of Northampton

The University of Northampton stands out for its commitment to social impact, earning multiple awards in this domain. Recognized as the UK's first Changemaker Campus in 2012, it focuses on transforming lives and inspiring change, both locally and globally. The university boasts a modern campus and aims to provide a fulfilling, satisfying, and enjoyable educational experience, underpinned by a mission to ensure students make a positive mark on society.

University of Northampton Website Link

London South Bank University

London South Bank University (LSBU) emphasizes practical education and employability, with a central London location that fosters close links to business and industry. Founded in 1892 and achieving university status in 1992, LSBU is among the largest universities in London, offering a diverse range of industry-relevant courses.
